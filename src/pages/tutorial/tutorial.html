Welcome to VR-Toby

VR-Toby is a deep machine learning system. Select a test and identify where the hearts displayed are enlarged or not. Your answers will help the AI learn.

What is a test?

A test consist of multiple cases that have previously been scored as being normal or enlarged.

Cases

Each case contains 1 or more x-rays that you will be asked to score. Tap the large x-ray in order to zoom and scroll to get a closer look. Before submitting your answer, if enlarged, please select any or all of the x-rays that led you to that conslusion.

Results

After completing the test you will be given your score to see how well you're able to detect cases of enlarged hearts.
